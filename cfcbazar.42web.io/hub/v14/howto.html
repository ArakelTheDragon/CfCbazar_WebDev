This is the WorkToken RPG game.

Follow these steps to start playing:

1. To get WorkTokens visit our WebMiner: https://cc.free.bg/site/miner/
2. Enter your email address.
3. Press Check WorkTokens.
4. Wait about 5 seconds until your WorkTokens are displayed.
5. Press Start Round.
6. Answer the question presented. (Hint: Based on the question, one of your set items might get updated — be careful what you answer!)
7. Discover hidden features and surprises on your own as you play.

Good luck and enjoy your adventure!
