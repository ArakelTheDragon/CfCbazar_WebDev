Cookie Policy

This site uses cookies to support session login and miner functionality.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your browser to remember who you are and how you use the site.

2. Types We Use

- Session Cookies: Keep you logged in
- Functional Cookies: Track mining activity and balance

3. Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. However, this may disrupt login and mining features.

4. Consent

By using CFCBazar, you agree to our use of cookies.
